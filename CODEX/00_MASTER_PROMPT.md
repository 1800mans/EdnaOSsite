# What EDNA OS Is

EDNA OS is **not** a startup.

It is **not** a SaaS product.

It is **not** an irrigation controller.

It is an engineering project establishing a new engineering category:

> **Orchard Operating System**

The website documents the engineering journey of designing, validating and continuously improving this category through real commercial orchard operations.

Visitors should immediately understand:

- this is a real engineering project
- this is being developed on a commercial orchard
- this is long-term research
- this is operational engineering
- this is continuous validation

The website should feel closer to an engineering laboratory than a software company's marketing page.

Engineering precedes software.

# Engineering Principles

Every engineering decision should support one or more of the following principles.

## Deterministic Control

Infrastructure should behave predictably under all operating conditions.

---

## Operational Evidence

Engineering decisions should be supported by measured field data rather than assumptions.

---

## Incremental Validation

New functionality is introduced gradually and validated under commercial operation.

---

## Layered Architecture

Independent system layers allow graceful degradation without complete system failure.

---

## Measurement Before Optimization

Optimization without measurement is speculation.

---

## Continuous Improvement

Every irrigation cycle provides operational evidence that improves future decision logic.

# Image Policy

During the development phase, high-quality AI-generated images may be used as engineering placeholders.

Requirements:

- Images must realistically depict commercial orchard infrastructure.
- Images should follow the Industrial Agriculture visual direction.
- Avoid futuristic, sci-fi, or marketing aesthetics.
- Avoid generic SaaS illustrations.
- Images should appear as realistic documentary photography.

Production Goal:

As the EDNA OS pilot orchard is developed, placeholder images will be systematically replaced with original engineering photographs.

All image filenames and paths must remain unchanged to allow seamless replacement without modifying application code.

All images follow the conventions defined in:
`05_IMAGE_CONVENTION.md`
