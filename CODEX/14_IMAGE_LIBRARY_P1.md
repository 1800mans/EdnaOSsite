# Purpose

This document defines the global photographic direction for the EDNA OS website.

It establishes the engineering standards, artistic direction, image composition, naming conventions and generation rules used throughout the project.

This document applies to every image in the EDNA OS image library.

# Image Philosophy

Images exist to communicate engineering reality.

They are not decoration.

They are not marketing.

They are not artistic illustrations.

Every image should help visitors understand that EDNA OS is engineered on a real commercial orchard.

---

# Visual Direction

The visual language is:

Industrial Agriculture

combined with

Automation

combined with

Built-in Research

The website should never resemble:

• SaaS

• Smart Farming

• Futuristic AI

• Consumer Electronics

• Laboratory Science

---

# Primary Goal

Every visitor should immediately understand:

This is a real engineering project.

This is real infrastructure.

This is a commercial orchard.

---

# Engineering Reality

Images should accurately represent:

commercial irrigation

electrical engineering

water management

orchard operations

field research

fertigation

# Photography Style

Preferred style

Ultra-realistic documentary photography.

Images should resemble professional editorial photography commissioned for an engineering magazine.

They should never resemble AI artwork.

# Lysimeter Rules

Extremely important.

The lysimeter should resemble agricultural research equipment.

It must NOT resemble

oil refinery

chemical reactor

wastewater treatment plant

fuel storage tank

industrial pressure vessel

laboratory reactor

A lysimeter should appear as

small underground engineering equipment

buried soil column

drainage collection

PVC piping

instrumentation

measurement

field research

The surrounding environment should clearly identify it as orchard infrastructure.

# Replacement Policy

Every AI-generated image is considered a placeholder.

The long-term objective is to replace every engineering photograph with original photography from the EDNA pilot orchard.

Replacement should require only overwriting image files.

No code changes.

---

# Icons

Preferred

Examples

Small feature highlights

Do not use photographs where icons communicate the idea more clearly.
